Lasagna

Ingredients

    1 1/2 lb. ground beef
    1 lb. hot breakfast sausage
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    2 14.5 oz. cans whole tomatoes
    2 6 oz. cans tomato paste
    4 Tbsp. dried parsley, divided
    2 Tbsp dried basil
    3 c. low-fat cottage cheese
    2 eggs, beaten
    1/2 c. grated (not shredded) parmesan cheese
    1 Tbsp. olive oil
    1 10 oz. package lasagna noodles
    1 lb. sliced mozzarella cheese

Steps

    Boil a large pot of water.
    Get a large skillet or saucean, combine ground beef, sausage, and garlic. Cook over medium-high heat unbtil browned. Drain half the fat. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, and 1 teaspoon salt. After adding the tomatoes, the sauce mixture should simmer for 45 minutes while you are working ont he other steps.
    In a medium bowl, mix cottage cheese, beaten eggs, grated parmesan, 2 more tablespoons parsley, and 1 more teaspoon salt. Stir together well. Set aside. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt and the olive oil to the boiling water, then cook the lasagna noodles until al dente and then drain.
    To assemble arrange 4 cooked lasagna noodles in the bottom of a baking pan, overlapping if necessary. Spoon half the cottage cheese mixture over the noodles. Spread evenly. Cover cottage cheese with a layer of mozzarella chesse. Spoon a little less than half the meat/ sauce micture over the top. Repeat, ending with meat/sauce mixture. Sprinkle top generously with extra parmesan.
    Either freeze or refrigerate for up to 2 days, or bake immediately at 350 degrees oven until the top is hot and bubbly, 20 to 30 minutes.
