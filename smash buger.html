Smash Burgers

Ingredients

    1.5 lbs of 80/20 ground beef to make 4 double-patty burgers.
    A simple combination of mayonnaise and yellow mustard
    Medium cheddar cheese
    Homemade buns
    Thinly sliced pickles
    Tomatoes
    Thinly sliced red onions

Steps

    Divide 1 1/2 lbs of gorund beef into 8 equal portions. Cover, refrigerate until ready to cook.
    Stir together 1/3 cup of mayo and 1 tsp mustard and set aside.
    Fully prep the toppings ahead to assemble the burgers right away while the patties and hot and juicy off the grill.
    Lightly butter the cut side of your buns and toast on a griddle or skillet ovcer mdeium heat until golden brown. Transfer to a rack.
    Increase the pan to a medium-high heat. Place 2 to 4 cold pieces of meat onto the hot suface. Working quickly, cover ech piece with a sheet of parchemnt paper and smash each into a thin patty. Remove parchment paper, season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder, add a dab of sauce, and flip. Sear for 2 minutes on the first side and about a minute on the second side.
    Place a slice of cheese onto half of the patties and top with a second paty and remove from heat. Serve warm over toasted buns with toppings.
