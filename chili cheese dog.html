Chili Cheese Dog

Ingredients

    Hot dog buns
    Hot dogs
    Onions
    Parsley
    Canned Chili

Steps

    Open hot dog buns and place them on a baking sheet.
    Cut onions into small pieces and place them on the side.
    Put the chili in a pot and cook on a medium heat for 10 minutes.
    Boil hot dogs in a medium pot until fully cooked.
    Put hot dogs in the buns and put chili and cheese on the hot dogs
    Send dogs on a baking sheet to the oven and bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes until the cheese is fully metled.
    Sprinkle parsley on hot dogs and serve.
